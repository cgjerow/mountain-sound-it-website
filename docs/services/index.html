Exploratory Development

If there is something that's been causing you problems in your daily business workflow, there is probably a solution. Not knowing what questions to ask or where to start should not keep you from solving the technical problems preventing you from giving your customers the best experiences possible.

With exploratory development I will work with you to understand the possibilties you have at your disposal, and prove out several possible solutions (with working software!) to get you started. I will evaluate options to avoid custom development all together whenever possible, as well as document custom solution designs so that I or any other application programmers can hit the ground running when you decide to build.

Managed Services

When technology isn't your business, managing a solution long term can be a hassle. Any solution I offer or existing solutions you own that don't receive ongoing care can be supported as a monthly or annual subscription. Under a managed services agreement you will retain an amount of time per month used for troubleshooting and maintaining your systems.
